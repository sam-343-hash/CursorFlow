Cursor Design Preview (real overlay.js / overlay.css, not a mockup)

Move your real mouse around the page first (so there's a "last known position" to glide from), then click any button below. Watch the red cursor glide from wherever your mouse last was to the button, with the glass-morphism label bubble. The amber button simulates a "sensitive action" (like the real extension would show for something like a delete/payment button - point only, no click).
